Who We Are

Multitenant properties, mortgages, cap rates, economic outlook, industrial space. With so much information - from types of property to ways to acquire and manage it, not to mention the plethora of macroeconomic factors affecting commercial real estate - it's easy to be overwhelmed. LORI Commercial Insider brings you the latest news and information on the Canadian commercial real estate market (Land, Office, Retail, and Industrial) in a easily digestible, elegant format. Available as a classic print magazine and accessible online; you'll never miss a thing.

Distributed across Ontario, LORI Commercial Insider reaches homes, businesses, and real estate offices.
